General
What is SuperTools? ▾

SuperTools is a free online file toolkit with 120+ tools for PDF, Image, Video, Audio, Documents, and Archives. Everything runs in your browser — no software to install, no account to create.

Is SuperTools really free? ▾

Yes, 100% free — no hidden fees, no subscriptions, no premium tiers. Every tool is fully accessible without paying anything. We are committed to keeping all tools free forever. See our Pricing page for more.

Do I need to create an account? ▾

No. You don't need to sign up, log in, or provide any personal information to use any tool on SuperTools. Just visit the website and start using the tools immediately.

How many tools does SuperTools have? ▾

SuperTools currently has 120+ tools across 6 categories: PDF (30+ tools), Image (28+ tools), Video (19+ tools), Audio (20+ tools), Documents (12+ tools), and Archives (9+ tools). We regularly add new tools.

Does SuperTools work on mobile? ▾

Yes! SuperTools is fully responsive and works on all devices — smartphones, tablets, and desktops. You don't need to install any app; just use it directly in your mobile browser.

Tools
Will my output files have watermarks? ▾

Never. SuperTools does not add any watermarks, logos, or branding to your output files. What you get is exactly what you uploaded — just processed the way you wanted.

Is there a limit on how many files I can process? ▾

No daily limits, no monthly quotas. You can process as many files as you need, as often as you like. All tools are unlimited.

What is the maximum file size I can upload? ▾

Most tools support files up to 100MB. Some tools like video converters may support larger files. If a file is too large, the tool will notify you with an appropriate message.

Which browsers are supported? ▾

SuperTools works best on modern browsers: Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, and Safari. We recommend keeping your browser up to date for the best experience.

Do I need to install anything? ▾

No installation required. SuperTools runs entirely in your web browser. Just open the website, choose your tool, and start working. No plugins, extensions, or software downloads needed.

Can I use SuperTools offline? ▾

Most tools require an active internet connection to load. However, some tools process files entirely in your browser, meaning once the page is loaded, the internet is not required to complete the task.

Privacy & Security
Are my files safe? Do you store them? ▾

Your privacy is our priority. Many of our tools process files directly in your browser — meaning your files never leave your device. For server-side tools, uploaded files are automatically deleted after processing. We do not store, share, or sell your files. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Do you track me or use cookies? ▾

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Any cookies used are strictly for basic website functionality. We do not sell your data to third parties. Read our Cookie Policy for more information.

Can I trust SuperTools with sensitive documents? ▾

Yes. We take security seriously. Files processed in-browser never touch our servers. For sensitive documents, we recommend using browser-based tools (indicated in the tool description) so your data stays on your device entirely.

Do you show ads? ▾

We may show minimal, non-intrusive ads to help keep the service free. We do not show pop-ups or misleading ads. Your user experience always comes first. SuperTools will always remain free regardless.

File Formats
What file formats does SuperTools support? ▾

SuperTools supports a wide range of formats including: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, TXT, RTF, EPUB, ODT, JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, SVG, HEIC, TIFF, RAW, ICO, MP4, AVI, MKV, MOV, WEBM, MP3, WAV, FLAC, AAC, OGG, WMA, M4A, ZIP, RAR, 7Z, TAR, GZ, and many more.

Can I convert multiple files at once? ▾

Some tools support batch processing (multiple files at once), such as Merge PDF or Image Compressor. The tool page will indicate if batch upload is supported. We are continuously adding batch support to more tools.

My converted file quality looks low. What can I do? ▾

Most tools have quality settings you can adjust before processing. Look for a "Quality" slider or settings panel within the tool. For best results, always start with a high-quality source file. If the issue persists, contact us.

Account & Support

We appreciate bug reports! Please visit our Contact page and describe the issue in detail — including the tool name, what you were doing, and the browser you are using. We will fix it as quickly as possible.

Can I suggest a new tool? ▾

Absolutely! We love hearing from users. Send your tool suggestions through our Contact page or reach out on Telegram. Popular requests get prioritized for the next update.

How do I stay updated on new tools? ▾

Follow us on Telegram or Twitter/X to get notified about new tools, updates, and announcements. We post regularly about new features.

Is there an affiliate or partnership program? ▾

Yes! SuperTools has an affiliate program. Read our Affiliate Policy to learn more about partnership opportunities and how you can earn by promoting SuperTools.
