Commission Structure

Commissions are calculated based on qualifying referrals. A qualifying referral is defined as a user who clicks your affiliate link and completes a qualifying action within the 90-day cookie window.

  • Standard Commission: Up to 30% on qualifying referrals
  • Bonus Tiers: Higher commission rates available for top-performing affiliates
  • Minimum Payout: $50 USD before monthly payment is processed
  • Payment Methods: PayPal or bank transfer (details provided upon approval)
📊 Commissions are tracked and visible in your affiliate dashboard in real-time.

Disclosure Requirements

All affiliates must clearly disclose their affiliate relationship with SuperTools in compliance with FTC guidelines and applicable advertising regulations. This means:

  • Clearly labeling affiliate links with text such as "affiliate link", "sponsored", or "I earn a commission"
  • Including a disclosure statement in any content that contains affiliate links
  • Not misrepresenting SuperTools products, features, or pricing
  • Not making false or misleading claims about our platform

Failure to disclose properly may result in immediate termination of your affiliate account.

The following promotional methods are strictly prohibited:
