Cookie Policy

We use cookies sparingly and responsibly. Here's a complete, transparent breakdown of every cookie we use and why.

Last Updated: January 2026
01

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, maintain session state, and gather anonymous statistics about usage.

Cookies are not executable programs and cannot carry viruses or malware. However, we understand you may want to control what's stored on your device — this policy explains exactly how.

🍪 SuperTools uses ONLY essential and anonymous analytics cookies. No advertising or tracking cookies.

03

Specific Cookies We Set

The following cookies may be placed when you use SuperTools:

  • _session — Session identifier for tool processing (expires when browser closes)
  • _analytics_id — Anonymous visitor ID for aggregate usage statistics (expires after 12 months)
  • _theme_pref — Remembers your display preferences if set (session cookie)
  • _cookie_consent — Stores your cookie consent choice (expires after 12 months)

No cookies contain personally identifiable information. All cookies follow the principle of data minimization.

04

Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services we use may set their own cookies:

  • Google Fonts — May set performance cookies for font delivery
  • Analytics Provider — Sets anonymous analytics cookies (no personal data)

We carefully vet all third-party services. None of our third-party services are permitted to use cookies for advertising or behavioral profiling on our platform.

05

How to Control Cookies

You have full control over cookies. Here's how to manage them in major browsers:

🌐 Chrome Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
🦊 Firefox Preferences → Privacy & Security
🧭 Safari Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
🔷 Edge Settings → Cookies & Site Permissions
⚠ Disabling essential cookies may prevent some tools from functioning correctly.
06

Your Consent

By continuing to use SuperTools, you consent to our use of essential cookies as described in this policy. For analytics cookies, you can opt out at any time using your browser settings or our cookie consent banner.

We respect "Do Not Track" (DNT) browser signals. When DNT is enabled, we disable analytics cookies automatically.
