Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Here's exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect you.

Last Updated: January 2026
01

Our Privacy Commitment

At SuperTools (gptpayer.online), privacy isn't an afterthought — it's a core principle. We built our platform so that powerful file tools can be used without sacrificing your personal data or privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our platform. We believe in full transparency, so we've written this in clear, plain language.

🔒 Your files are never stored on our servers. All processing happens in-browser or in temporary memory and is deleted immediately after completion.
02

File Processing & Storage

When you upload a file to SuperTools, here is exactly what happens:

  • Files are processed client-side (in your browser) or in temporary server memory
  • Processed files are available for download for a very short time, then automatically deleted
  • We do not store, read, analyze, or share your uploaded files
  • No human at gptpayer.online ever sees your file content
  • Files are encrypted in transit using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS

04

Cookies & Tracking

We use only essential and analytics cookies. We do not use cookies for advertising, remarketing, or behavioral profiling.

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous, aggregate data about platform usage (no personal identifiers)
  • No advertising cookies: We never serve targeted ads or sell data to advertisers

For full details on cookies, see our Cookie Policy.

05

Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate SuperTools:

  • Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics (no personally identifiable data shared)
  • CDN / Hosting: Fast and secure file delivery infrastructure
  • Google Fonts: For typography loading (subject to Google's privacy policy)

We never sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party under any circumstances.

06

Children's Privacy

SuperTools is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately at gptpayer@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

07

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Right to deletion: Request removal of any personal data we may hold
  • Right to opt-out: Opt out of analytics tracking via your browser settings
  • Right to portability: Receive your data in a commonly-used format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at gptpayer@gmail.com.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.

📬 For significant changes, we may notify users via a notice on our homepage.
