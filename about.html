Who We Are

SuperTools by gptpayer.online is a free, all-in-one file conversion and editing platform built for everyone. From students and freelancers to developers and businesses — our tools are designed to save you time and eliminate frustration.

We started with a simple idea: why should people pay for basic file conversion? Our team built SuperTools to solve exactly that. Today, we offer 120+ browser-based tools that run securely without storing any of your data on our servers.

Everything is processed locally in your browser or deleted immediately after conversion — your files remain 100% private.
